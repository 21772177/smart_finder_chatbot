## 🔗 Connect Your Accounts

### Why Connect?

To search your **saved content** from YouTube, Instagram, and Facebook, you need to connect your accounts.

### How to Connect:

1. **Click "Connect YouTube"** button
   - A popup window opens
   - Log into your YouTube account
   - Click "Allow" to grant access
   - ✅ YouTube connected!

2. **Click "Connect Instagram"** button
   - A popup window opens
   - Log into your Instagram account
   - Click "Allow" to grant access
   - ✅ Instagram connected!

3. **Click "Connect Facebook"** button
   - A popup window opens
   - Log into your Facebook account
   - Click "Allow" to grant access
   - ✅ Facebook connected!

**That's it!** No API keys, no technical setup, just click and authorize!

## ❓ Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: Do I need to create API keys?
**A:** No! The chatbot handles all technical setup. Just connect your accounts.

### Q: Is my data safe?
**A:** Yes! Your access tokens are stored securely. Only you can access your accounts.

### Q: Can I disconnect my accounts?
**A:** Yes! You can revoke access from your platform settings (YouTube, Instagram, Facebook).

### Q: Do I need to pay anything?
**A:** No! The chatbot is free to use.

### Q: What if I don't connect any accounts?
**A:** You can still search public content, but you won't be able to search your saved content.

If you encounter any issues:

Monetization Strategy - Smart Finder AI Chatbot

1) Freemium
 - Free tier: limited queries per day, basic platforms
 - Pro tier: unlimited queries, priority results, premium connectors

2) Affiliate / Commission
 - Use affiliate links for food delivery or bookings (Zomato/Swiggy/MakeMyTrip)

3) Sponsored Listings
 - Local businesses can pay to appear in promoted slots (clearly labeled)

4) B2B Licensing
 - Offer a white-label chatbot to hotels, malls, or city portals

5) API Access
 - Provide your own API to partners for a fee (per call model)

# 📍 Where to Fill App Name & Details - Visual Guide

## 🎯 You're Currently On:

**Page:** OAuth Overview (Google Auth Platform)  
**What you see:** Metrics, traffic, errors, users, token grant rate

**❌ App name and details are NOT on this page!**

---

## ✅ Where to Go:

**Page:** OAuth Consent Screen (APIs & Services)  
**This is where you fill:** App name, email, home page, privacy policy, etc.

---

## 🚀 Fastest Way: Direct Link

**Click this link:**
```
https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/credentials/consent?project=buildkit-1695f
```

**This takes you directly to the form where you add:**
- ✅ App name
- ✅ User support email  
- ✅ Application home page
- ✅ Privacy policy link
- ✅ Authorized domains
- ✅ Developer contact

---

## 🧭 How to Navigate from Current Page

### Step-by-Step:

1. **Look at top left corner** - You'll see a **hamburger menu icon** (☰)
2. **Click the hamburger menu** (☰)
3. **Scroll down** in the menu
4. **Find "APIs & Services"** section
5. **Click "APIs & Services"**
6. **In the left sidebar** (after clicking APIs & Services), you'll see:
   - Dashboard
   - Library
   - **OAuth consent screen** ← **Click this!**
   - Credentials
   - etc.
7. **Click "OAuth consent screen"**
8. **You're there!** Now you'll see the form to fill app name and details
